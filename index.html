PEPPA PIG

Character Information

Peppa Pig is the lead character in the show "Peppa Pig". She loves jumping up and down in muddy puddles along with her family. Peppa loves going on adventures with her friends aswell as going to "playgroup".

George is Peppa's little brother who loves his dinosaur toy called Mr. Dinosaur. George is often there to remind Peppa of her older sister duties especially when he forgets his boots when jumping up and down in muddy puddles.

George and Mr. Dinosaur

Mummy Pig is Peppa's mum, She's very sweet and takes on a motherly role to both Peppa and George, making sure to guide them on whatever they need.

Mummy Pig and Peppa

Daddy Pig, often made fun of for his big tummy, is Peppa's Dad. He takes on a very fatherly example to both Peppa and George. Even if he likes watching TV all day, he still makes sure that he can do everything aslong as its for Peppa and George.
